Technology of Interactions is a series of home appliances investigating our relationships with data and technology: ToI want to keep us connected without the associated anxiety and emotional baggage.
Incorporating modern technologies into humble packages, the appliances are designed to be invisible and blend into our daily lives. ToI requires minimal amounts of direct interactions with human users, adopting a non-interfering approach to inform us of the worlds we live in.
